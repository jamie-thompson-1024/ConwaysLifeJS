Conways Game of Life

About

John Conway's Game of Life is a well know cellular automaton algorithm. It demonstraits complex systems emerging from a simple set of rules applied to a grid.

Rules

Rules:

  • A live cell with less than 2 neighbors dies
  • A live cell with 2-3 neighbors survives
  • A live cell with more than 3 neighbots dies
  • A dead cell with 3 neighbors becomes a live cell
